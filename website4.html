About Rajagiri

Rajagiri College of Social Sciences (RCSS), established in 1955, is one of the pioneering higher education institutions in India, located in Kochi, Kerala. The campus is known for its world-class infrastructure, research-driven environment, and community outreach programs. Rooted in the Carmelite vision of education, Rajagiri has a tradition of molding students into responsible leaders who combine academic excellence with compassion.

The college offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in fields like management, social work, computer science, psychology, and commerce. It has consistently ranked among the top colleges in India for its strong academic standards, holistic student development, and industry partnerships.

Fun fact: Rajagiri’s motto is "Learn, Serve, Excel," which reflects its mission of blending learning with social responsibility.

Campus Life

Life at Rajagiri goes beyond classrooms. Students actively participate in cultural festivals, inter-collegiate competitions, and volunteering programs. The sprawling green campus hosts multiple student clubs, research cells, and innovation labs. Every year, task fests like INFLORE (Management Fest), Rajagiri National Business Quiz, and cultural extravaganzas bring together talent from across the nation.

The college is also known for its unique Rajagiri Outreach Program, where students engage with rural communities, organize health camps, and implement sustainable development projects. This hands-on exposure helps students build empathy and leadership skills.

Events & Highlights

Rajagiri hosts numerous academic, cultural, and social events. From international conferences to food festivals and cultural exchange programs, students experience a vibrant academic life. Popular highlights include:

  • Annual Convocation Ceremony celebrating academic excellence
  • INFLORE – South India’s biggest management fest
  • Rajagiri National Business Quiz with participation from top B-schools
  • Social Outreach task campaigns impacting local communities
