TechNova is an annual tech task innovation festival that brings together aspiring technologists, engineers, and designers from across the country. With workshops, hackathons, and live demonstrations, participants explore cutting-edge technologies and showcase innovative task solutions. This year, we have added virtual participation options for students worldwide, making collaboration easier than ever. Each workshop is treated as a mini-task to develop skills faster.
From AI-driven robotics challenges to immersive VR experiences, TechNova fosters creativity, teamwork, and problem-solving. Our festival features keynote sessions from industry leaders, interactive coding task competitions, and hands-on tech labs to fuel curiosity and learning. New mini-events this year include drone races, IoT hack labs, and AI puzzle challenges. Each challenge comes with a small task for participants.

Innovators Institute of Technology

Innovators Institute of Technology (IITech) is renowned for its excellence in engineering education and research. Established in 1998, IITech combines world-class faculty, modern labs, and a culture of innovation to empower students to become leaders in technology. Our motto, "Engineering the Future," reflects our commitment to shaping innovators who can tackle real-world task challenges with creativity and skill. New student-led initiatives include coding clubs, robotics societies, and AI research labs.
Join the Robotics Workshop at TechNova 2025 to build intelligent robots, learn about sensors and AI, and compete in thrilling robotics task challenges. Additional mini-tasks include coding obstacle-avoiding robots and automated line-followers.
