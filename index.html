Round 2 - Rules and Guidelines

Round 2: Task Completion

In this round, you will need to complete a total of 10 tasks. Please follow these detailed steps to successfully navigate through the round:

  1. Start with the Provided URL: You will receive an initial URL that will be your starting point. Visit this URL to begin.
  2. Search for the First Task: Each webpage you visit will contain a hidden task. Thoroughly explore every part of the page, including text, images, links, and any other elements. The task can be located anywhere on the page, so be diligent in your search.
  3. Complete the Task: Once you locate the task, carefully follow the instructions provided to complete it. Ensure you understand the task requirements and fulfill them accurately.
  4. Update Your Progress: After completing each task, update the status in the WhatsApp group created for your team.
  5. Complete the Tasks in Order: It is crucial to complete the tasks in the order they are presented. Do not skip any tasks or attempt them out of sequence.
  6. Move to the Next Webpage: After completing a task, you will need to proceed to the next webpage to find the subsequent task. This new page may be provided directly or could require you to navigate through links or clues.
  7. Find and Complete the Next Task: On each new webpage, repeat the search process to find the hidden task. Be thorough in your exploration to ensure you don't miss any clues.
  8. Handling Dummy Pages: Some webpages may be dummy pages with no tasks or clues. If you encounter a dummy page, return to the previous webpage and search for an alternative URL or path that will lead you to the correct page with the task.
