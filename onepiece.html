ALL ONE PIECE ARCS

A comprehensive guide to every major arc, from the start of the journey to the final saga.

Romance Dawn Arc

+
Episodes: 1-3 | Manga Chapters: 1-7

The story begins with a young, idealistic pirate named Monkey D. Luffy, who has a rubber body from eating a Devil Fruit. His goal is to become the King of the Pirates by finding the legendary treasure, the One Piece. This arc introduces him as he begins his journey and recruits his first crewmate, the formidable swordsman Roronoa Zoro.

Orange Town Arc

+
Episodes: 4-8 | Manga Chapters: 8-21

In Orange Town, Luffy and Zoro encounter Buggy the Clown, a pirate with the ability to split his body into pieces. They also meet Nami, a cunning navigator and a skilled thief who initially seeks to rob them, but is forced to ally with them to defeat Buggy.

Syrup Village Arc

+
Episodes: 9-18 | Manga Chapters: 22-41

The crew lands in Syrup Village, where they meet Usopp, a talented sniper and notorious liar. The arc focuses on Usopp's struggle to convince the villagers of a real pirate threat, as Luffy and his crew must defend the village against the ruthless pirate Captain Kuro and his Black Cat Pirates.

Baratie Arc

+
Episodes: 19-30 | Manga Chapters: 42-68

In search of a cook, the Straw Hats arrive at the Baratie, a floating restaurant. They meet Sanji, a chivalrous and powerful chef. The arc culminates in a major battle against the pirate fleet of Don Krieg and introduces the world's greatest swordsman, Dracule Mihawk.

Arlong Park Arc

+
Episodes: 31-44 | Manga Chapters: 69-95

The crew follows Nami to her hometown, where they discover her tragic past under the tyrannical rule of the fish-man pirate Arlong. Luffy's famous cry for help and his decisive battle against Arlong officially cements Nami's loyalty and her place as a true Straw Hat Pirate.

Loguetown Arc

+
Episodes: 45, 48-53 | Manga Chapters: 96-100

The crew makes a final stop at Loguetown, the "town of the beginning and the end," where the Pirate King Gold Roger was born and executed. They face a new enemy, Captain Smoker, and witness a miraculous event that solidifies Luffy's destiny before they set off into the Grand Line.

Reverse Mountain Arc

+
Episodes: 62-63 | Manga Chapters: 101-105

The Straw Hats finally enter the Grand Line by navigating the dangerous Reverse Mountain. At the entrance, they meet the giant whale Laboon and a strange lighthouse keeper who guides them on their first steps into the new sea.

Whiskey Peak Arc

+
Episodes: 64-67 | Manga Chapters: 106-114

The crew lands on a seemingly friendly island that turns out to be a town of bounty hunters. Luffy and Zoro have a comical misunderstanding, but the arc is most significant for revealing Princess Vivi and her mission to save her kingdom from the criminal organization Baroque Works.

Little Garden Arc

+
Episodes: 70-77 | Manga Chapters: 115-129

A detour leads the crew to a prehistoric island where they encounter two giants, Dorry and Brogy, who have been fighting for 100 years. The Straw Hats must survive the island's dangers and a new threat from Baroque Works agents.

Drum Island Arc

+
Episodes: 78-91 | Manga Chapters: 130-154

The crew's search for a doctor for an ill Nami takes them to Drum Island, a snowy country oppressed by a tyrant. They meet Tony Tony Chopper, a kind-hearted reindeer who dreams of becoming a doctor, and fight to free the island, gaining a new crewmate.

Alabasta Arc

+
Episodes: 92-130 | Manga Chapters: 155-217

Luffy and his crew officially ally with Princess Vivi to stop a civil war manipulated by the Warlord Crocodile. This is their first grand-scale battle, where each crewmate faces a powerful Baroque Works agent. The arc concludes with a tearful farewell and the unexpected addition of Nico Robin to the crew.

Jaya Arc

+
Episodes: 144-152 | Manga Chapters: 218-236

The crew arrives at a lawless island where they encounter a pirate who mocks them for believing in dreams. They learn of an island in the sky and prepare to sail for it, setting the stage for their next great adventure.

Skypiea Arc

+
Episodes: 153-195 | Manga Chapters: 237-302

The Straw Hats ascend into the sky and arrive at the mythical Sky Island. Here, they must fight against the self-proclaimed god, Enel, a Devil Fruit user with the power of lightning. The arc delves into themes of history and fate, and the crew discovers a massive treasure.

Long Ring Long Land Arc

+
Episodes: 207-219 | Manga Chapters: 303-321

The crew encounters the Foxy Pirates and is forced to play a series of bizarre pirate games known as the "Davy Back Fight." This comical arc introduces the Admiral Aokiji, who challenges Luffy and severely defeats him, showing the true power of the Marines.

Water 7 Arc

+
Episodes: 227-263 | Manga Chapters: 322-374

The Straw Hats arrive at Water 7, a beautiful city of shipwrights, to repair the Going Merry. This arc is a turning point, with major internal conflicts as Usopp leaves the crew and Nico Robin mysteriously betrays them. The crew meets Franky, a cyborg shipwright who will become a key ally.

Enies Lobby Arc

+
Episodes: 264-312 | Manga Chapters: 375-430

The Straw Hats declare war on the World Government by burning their flag and storm the judicial island of Enies Lobby. They fight the intelligence agency CP9 to rescue Robin, culminating in a powerful battle where the crew demonstrates their resolve to fight for their friends, no matter the cost.

Thriller Bark Arc

+
Episodes: 337-381 | Manga Chapters: 442-490

The crew enters the mysterious and spooky Florian Triangle and finds themselves on the world's largest ship, Thriller Bark. They must fight zombies and reclaim their shadows from the Warlord, Gecko Moria, and a powerful enemy, Bartholomew Kuma.

Sabaody Archipelago Arc

+
Episodes: 385-405 | Manga Chapters: 491-513

The Straw Hats land at the Sabaody Archipelago and encounter other infamous rookie pirates. They make a critical mistake by punching a Celestial Dragon, attracting a powerful Marine Admiral and Kuma, who single-handedly defeat and separate the crew, sending them to different locations around the world.

Amazon Lily Arc

+
Episodes: 408-417 | Manga Chapters: 514-524

Luffy is sent flying to an all-female island and finds himself in a kingdom ruled by the beautiful and powerful Pirate Empress, Boa Hancock. He learns of his brother Ace's impending execution, and Hancock agrees to help him infiltrate the great prison of Impel Down.

Impel Down Arc

+
Episodes: 422-452 | Manga Chapters: 525-548

Luffy infiltrates the world's greatest underwater prison to rescue his brother. He forms alliances with former enemies, including Buggy the Clown and Crocodile, and faces the formidable warden Magellan in a desperate race against time.

Marineford Arc

+
Episodes: 459-489 | Manga Chapters: 549-581

The world is turned upside down in an epic war between the Whitebeard Pirates and the Marines to save Ace. This arc features a clash of titans and is a dramatic, high-stakes battle that leaves a permanent mark on the series and its characters, as Luffy suffers a devastating loss.

Return to Sabaody Arc

+
Episodes: 517-522 | Manga Chapters: 598-602

Following a two-year time skip, the crew reunites at Sabaody Archipelago, all of them having trained and grown significantly stronger. They easily defeat a fake Straw Hat crew and set off for the New World.

Fish-Man Island Arc

+
Episodes: 523-574 | Manga Chapters: 603-653

The crew travels to an underwater kingdom to fulfill a promise. They fight against a group of fish-man extremists who threaten the country's peace and learn more about the world's tumultuous history.

Punk Hazard Arc

+
Episodes: 579-625 | Manga Chapters: 654-699

The Straw Hats land on a mysterious island split between ice and fire. They form an alliance with Trafalgar Law to take down one of the Four Emperors, Kaido, and they face off against a powerful villain with a devastating gas ability.

Dressrosa Arc

+
Episodes: 629-746 | Manga Chapters: 700-801

The crew, along with their new alliance, fights to liberate the country of Dressrosa from the tyrannical rule of Donquixote Doflamingo. This is a large-scale battle with a wide cast of characters, political intrigue, and spectacular battles that bring the crew's power to a new level.

Zou Arc

+
Episodes: 751-779 | Manga Chapters: 802-822

The crew meets the Mink tribe, who live on the back of a giant elephant. They discover the new alliance that has formed and learn about the Road Poneglyphs, ancient texts that can lead them to the One Piece. This arc is a setup for the next major conflict.

Whole Cake Island Arc

+
Episodes: 783-877 | Manga Chapters: 825-902

A desperate rescue mission to retrieve Sanji from the clutches of one of the Four Emperors, Big Mom. A small team of the Straw Hats must navigate the fantastical, sweet-filled territory and form a temporary alliance with a former enemy. This arc is a thrilling cat-and-mouse game against an unstoppable force, filled with family drama and betrayal.

Wano Country Arc

+
Episodes: 890-1085 | Manga Chapters: 909-1057

The longest and most climactic saga to date. Luffy and a massive alliance of pirates, samurai, and ninjas team up to liberate the isolated country of Wano from two of the Four Emperors, Kaido and Big Mom. This arc is a grand culmination of many long-running plotlines and features some of the most spectacular fights in the series' history.

Egghead Island Arc

+
Episodes: 1086-present | Manga Chapters: 1060-present

The crew enters the final saga of the series. They land on a futuristic island of advanced technology and meet the brilliant scientist, Dr. Vegapunk. This arc is currently ongoing and is revealing major secrets and lore that will lead to the final great war.
