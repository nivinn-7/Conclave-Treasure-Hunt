Romance Dawn Arc

Episodes: 1-3 | Manga Chapters: 1-7

The story begins with Monkey D. Luffy, who dreams of becoming Pirate King. A crucial task here is the introduction of Zoro. This is where the adventure truly begins.

Orange Town Arc

Episodes: 4-8 | Manga Chapters: 8-21

In Orange Town, Luffy and Zoro encounter Buggy the Clown, a pirate with the ability to split his body into pieces. They also meet Nami, a cunning navigator and a skilled thief who initially seeks to rob them, but is forced to ally with them to defeat Buggy.

Syrup Village Arc

Episodes: 9-18 | Manga Chapters: 22-41

The crew lands in Syrup Village, where they meet Usopp, a talented sniper and notorious liar. The arc focuses on Usopp's struggle to convince the villagers of a real pirate threat, as Luffy and his crew must defend the village against the ruthless pirate Captain Kuro and his Black Cat Pirates.

Baratie Arc

Episodes: 19-30 | Manga Chapters: 42-68

In search of a cook, the Straw Hats arrive at the Baratie, a floating restaurant. They meet Sanji, a chivalrous and powerful chef. The arc culminates in a major battle against the pirate fleet of Don Krieg and introduces the world's greatest swordsman, Dracule Mihawk.

Arlong Park Arc

Episodes: 31-44 | Manga Chapters: 69-95

This is one of the most emotional arcs where Nami’s past is revealed. The task of defeating Arlong is one of Luffy’s earliest big victories.

Marineford Arc

Episodes: 459-489 | Manga Chapters: 549-581

The task here is survival. This war brings together Marines, Warlords, and the Whitebeard Pirates. It changes the world forever.
